Getting Started

Voice Journal is your personal audio diary. Speak your thoughts, and the app automatically transcribes, analyzes your mood, detects to‑dos, and sets reminders to help you stay on track.

  • Tap Record to start a new entry.
  • Stop to auto‑transcribe and generate a summary, mood, and suggested to‑dos.
  • Review detected to‑dos and add reminders if you want.
  • Browse past entries and tasks in the timeline and to‑dos tabs.

What You Can Do

  • Daily voice journaling: Capture thoughts quickly by speaking.
  • Smart tasks: Automatically generate action items from your entries.
  • Reminders: Set notifications to follow through on to‑dos.
  • Organized timeline: Review past entries, summaries, and tasks in one place.

FAQ

How does transcription and analysis work?

Voice Journal can run on‑device transcription and analysis. If you configure an optional backend in settings, the app may securely send audio or transcripts to that backend to generate summaries, mood, and to‑dos.

Where is my data stored?

By default, your entries and to‑dos are stored locally on your device. If a backend is enabled, it may temporarily process your data to return results. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Delete individual entries and to‑dos or clear all app data from settings. If you use a backend, deletion there depends on your server’s policy.

Reminders aren’t firing. What should I check?

Ensure notifications are allowed for Voice Journal in your device settings and verify your reminder times inside the app.

Does it work offline?

Yes, you can record and review entries offline. Cloud analysis (if enabled) requires internet access.
