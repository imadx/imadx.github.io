Getting Started

Learn Terraform focuses on practicing multiple‑choice questions (MCQs) to help you reinforce key Terraform concepts. There are no lessons or tracks in this version — it’s all about quick practice.

Open the question list, pick a question, view the answer choices, select an option, and then proceed to the next question. You can review previously viewed questions from the past questions list.

What You Can Do

  • Browse Questions: View a list of available MCQ questions.
  • View Answers: Tap a question to see the multiple‑choice answers.
  • Select an Answer: Choose an option and continue to the next question.
  • Past Questions: Revisit previously viewed questions from the past questions list.

Frequently Asked Questions

What features are included?

This app currently supports MCQ practice only: browsing questions, viewing choices, selecting an answer, moving to the next question, and reviewing past questions. There are no tracks, lessons, or progress tracking in this version.

Can I review past questions?

Yes. Use the past questions list to revisit previously viewed questions.

How often is the app updated?

We ship regular content improvements and fixes. Check your app store for updates.

How is my data used?

We do not collect analytics or personal data. No usage or device information is sent to us. See our Privacy Policy for details.
